Spain 6 Days & 5 Nights Itinerary

Explore the vibrant culture, architecture, and cuisine of Spain with this exciting itinerary.

Day 1: Arrival in Barcelona

Arrive in the vibrant city of Barcelona. Check into your hotel and explore La Rambla and the Gothic Quarter for an authentic Spanish experience.

Day 2: Gaudí's Masterpieces

Visit iconic sites like the Sagrada Família, Park Güell, and Casa Batlló. Enjoy a traditional Spanish dinner at a local tapas bar.

Day 3: Seville's Charm

Travel to Seville, known for its flamenco dancing and the stunning Alcázar. Visit Seville Cathedral and take a horse-drawn carriage ride.

Day 4: Granada and Alhambra

Explore the magnificent Alhambra Palace and Generalife Gardens. Wander through the Albaicín district for stunning views and Moorish charm.

Day 5: Madrid Highlights

Visit Madrid, the capital of Spain. Explore the Royal Palace, Prado Museum, and Retiro Park. End the day with a traditional Spanish fiesta.

Day 6: Departure

Enjoy a leisurely breakfast before departing from Madrid. Take home unforgettable memories of Spain's beauty and culture.
