Greece 6 Days & 5 Nights Itinerary

Discover the breathtaking beauty, history, and charm of Greece with this exclusive itinerary.

Day 1: Arrival in Athens

Arrive in the historic city of Athens. Check into your hotel and visit the Acropolis and Plaka district for an introduction to Greek history and culture.

Day 2: Explore Athens

Spend the day visiting the National Archaeological Museum, Syntagma Square, and the Temple of Olympian Zeus. Enjoy a traditional Greek dinner with live music.

Day 3: Santorini

Travel to Santorini and marvel at its stunning white-washed architecture and blue-domed churches. Explore Oia for its iconic sunset views.

Day 4: Caldera Cruise

Take a cruise around the caldera, visit the volcanic islands, and relax at the hot springs. Enjoy a seafood dinner by the sea.

Enjoy a relaxing morning in Mykonos before your departure. Take with you memories of Greece's beauty and charm.
