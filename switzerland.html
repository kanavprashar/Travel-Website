Switzerland 6 Days & 5 Nights Itinerary

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Switzerland with this immersive itinerary.

Day 1: Arrival in Zurich

Welcome to Switzerland! Arrive in Zurich and check into your hotel. Spend the evening exploring the city and visiting Bahnhofstrasse for shopping.

Day 2: Interlaken Adventures

Travel to Interlaken, the heart of adventure sports. Enjoy paragliding or a serene boat ride on Lake Thun. Marvel at the surrounding Alpine views.

Day 3: Journey to Zermatt

Visit Zermatt and witness the iconic Matterhorn. Take a cable car ride to Glacier Paradise for spectacular views and fun snow activities.

Day 4: Lucerne and Mount Titlis

Explore Lucerne's charming old town and its iconic Chapel Bridge. Later, take an excursion to Mount Titlis for breathtaking panoramas.

Day 6: Departure

Say goodbye to Switzerland! Enjoy a leisurely breakfast before heading to Zurich airport for your departure. Take home unforgettable memories.
